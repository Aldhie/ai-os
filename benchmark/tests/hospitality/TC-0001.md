## Question

```
Role: You are the AI assistant for a 5-star hotel.
Guest message:

"I checked in 3 hours ago and my room still hasn't been cleaned. 
I asked housekeeping twice already and nothing happened. 
I have an important business meeting in my room in 1 hour. 
This is completely unacceptable for a hotel of this caliber."

Respond as the hotel's AI assistant. Handle this complaint completely.
```

---

## Expected Behaviour

1. **Immediate acknowledgment** with genuine empathy (not scripted "I understand your frustration")
2. **Ownership** — does not blame housekeeping or make excuses
3. **Concrete action** — offers specific resolution: priority cleaning dispatch, timeline (e.g., "within 20 minutes"), and compensation offer (upgrade, complimentary service, or discount)
4. **Addresses the meeting constraint** — offers alternative space (boardroom, lounge) if 20 min is not enough for 1-hour meeting window
5. **Follow-up commitment** — personal follow-up from manager or specific contact
6. **Tone**: Warm, professional, not defensive, not over-apologetic

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Genuine empathy (not canned) | 20% | Natural, specific, not templated |
| Concrete resolution timeline | 25% | Specific action + time estimate |
| Addresses meeting constraint | 25% | Offers alternative space or meeting guarantee |
| Compensation offer | 15% | Tangible service recovery gesture |
| Professional warm tone | 15% | Not defensive, not over-apologetic |

---

## Scoring

| Score | Condition |
|-------|-----------|
| 10/10 | All 5 criteria met; addresses meeting proactively |
| 8/10 | Empathy + resolution + compensation; misses meeting constraint |
| 6/10 | Empathy + resolution; no compensation or meeting offer |
| 4/10 | Generic apology with vague resolution |
| 2/10 | Canned response; no specific action |
| 0/10 | Defensive, dismissive, or blaming |

---

## Failure Conditions

- Blames housekeeping staff
- Makes excuses ("we are very busy today")
- Does not offer any compensation or service recovery
- Ignores the 1-hour meeting constraint
