## Question

```
You deployed a config change to your Open WebUI instance yesterday.
Before: responses were detailed, coherent, well-structured.
After: responses are short, repetitive, and sometimes cut off mid-sentence.

The diff between old and new config:
- temperature: 1.0 → 0.1
- top_p: 0.95 → 0.95 (unchanged)
- max_tokens: 4096 → 256
- repetition_penalty: null → 1.8
- stream: true → true (unchanged)

User reports: "The AI now just repeats itself and stops after 2 sentences."

Diagnose which config change(s) caused each symptom:
1. Responses are short (2 sentences)
2. Responses are repetitive
3. Responses cut off mid-sentence

For each symptom, explain the mechanism and prescribe the correct fix.
```

---

## Expected Behaviour

1. **Short responses** → `max_tokens: 256` is the direct cause. 256 tokens ≈ 190 words ≈ 2-3 sentences. Fix: restore to 2048+.
2. **Repetitive responses** → `repetition_penalty: 1.8` is too aggressive — heavily penalizes any repeated token, causing the model to avoid re-using words in natural ways, paradoxically producing short loops. Combined with low temperature, the model gets stuck in local minima. Fix: set to 1.0 (disabled) or at most 1.1.
3. **Cut off mid-sentence** → `max_tokens: 256` causes hard truncation at token boundary regardless of sentence completion. Fix: increase max_tokens.
4. **Bonus insight**: `temperature: 0.1` combined with `repetition_penalty: 1.8` creates a particularly bad interaction — extremely low temperature already reduces diversity; adding repetition_penalty on top causes degenerate outputs.
