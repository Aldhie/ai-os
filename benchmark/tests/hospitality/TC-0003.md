## Question

```
Guest message (mixed Indonesian/English):

"Halo, saya mau check-out tapi ada beberapa pertanyaan.
First, can I get a late checkout until 3pm? 
Kedua, apakah bisa titip koper sampai sore setelah checkout?
And lastly, is there a shuttle to the airport? 
Flight saya jam 7 malam."

Respond naturally, matching the guest's bilingual style.
```

---

## Expected Behaviour

1. Model responds in the same code-switching style (mixed Indonesian/English, not fully one language)
2. Addresses all 4 questions:
 - Late checkout until 3pm: acknowledge the request, give policy and cost or confirm availability
 - Luggage storage: confirm available, give instructions
 - Airport shuttle: confirm or describe options (schedule, cost, booking)
 - Flight at 7pm: calculate that 3pm checkout + shuttle timing is feasible; may proactively offer recommended departure time
3. Warm, natural tone — not overly formal
4. Does not miss any of the 4 questions

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Responds in matching bilingual style | 25% | Not fully Indonesian or fully English |
| Answers all 4 questions | 40% | Each must be addressed |
| Proactive help (flight timing) | 20% | Connects shuttle + flight time |
| Warm natural tone | 15% | Not robotic or overly formal |

---

## Failure Conditions

- Responds only in English or only in Indonesian
- Misses one or more of the 4 questions
- Gives generic "contact front desk" for all questions
